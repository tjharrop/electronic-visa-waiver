Apply for an electronic visa waiver

If you have a passport from Qatar, the United Arab Emirates, Kuwait or Oman you can use this service to apply for an electronic visa waiver (EVW) to travel to the UK.

You can you EVW to make one visit to the UK for up to 6 months as a tourist, for a business trip or to study. There is no charge for an EVW.

Important information

You need to carefully complete an EVW application at least 48 hours before you travel to the UK.

The personal details you provide in your EVW application must match those in your passport exactly.

If the information you enter is not exactly as in your passport, you may not be allowed to board your flight.

You will need to apply for another EVW if:

  • your travel times, dates or details change
  • you plan to visit the UK again

If you are travelling as part of a group, each group member will need to apply for an EVW.

Uploading an image of your passport picture page

If you have an image of your passport picture page, we ask you to upload it to provide you with a better, quicker service.

With this image we will:

  • check the information you have provided matches the information on your passport
  • if we find an issue we will be able to contact you to correct it

You can use the service without providing an image of your passport, but if there is an issue it may take longer to correct it.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • your passport
  • a photo or scan of your passport
  • your final travel details to and from the UK
  • the address in the UK where you will be staying
