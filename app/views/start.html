Electronic visa waiver

If you have a passport from Qatar, the United Arab Emirates or Oman you can travel to the UK using an electronic visa waiver.

You can use an electronic visa waiver (EVW) to visit the UK for up to 6 months as a tourist, for a business trip or to study. There is no charge for an EVW.

Important information

Your EVW lets you travel to the UK and stay for up to 6 months. If you plan to visit the UK again you will need to complete a new form.

You need to complete it at least 48 hours before you travel. But, if your travel details are final, you can complete it up to 3 months before you travel.

If your travel times, dates or details change you will need to complete a new form.

If you are traveling with family members, you need to fill in a form for each person.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • your passport and those of any family members travelling with you
  • a photo or scan of your passport
  • your final travel details to and from the UK
  • the address in the UK where you will be staying
