Apply for an electronic visa waiver

If you have a passport from Qatar, the United Arab Emirates or Oman you can use this service to apply for an electronic visa waiver (EVW) to travel to the UK.

You can make one visit to the UK for up to 6 months as a tourist, for a business trip or to study. There is no charge for an EVW.

Important information

You need to carefully complete an EVW application at least 48 hours before you travel to the UK.

You need to make sure:

  • the personal details you provide in your EVW application match those in your passport exactly
  • you provide your final travel details

You will need to apply for another EVW if:

  • your travel times, dates or details change
  • you plan to visit the UK again

If you are travelling as part of a group, each group member will need to apply for an EVW.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • your passport
  • a photo or scan of your passport
  • your final travel details to and from the UK
  • the address in the UK where you will be staying
