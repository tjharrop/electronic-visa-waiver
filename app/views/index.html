Electronic Visa Waiver

You may be entitled to complete an Electronic Visa Waiver to enter the UK.

An Electronic Visa Waiver:

  • is valid for a single visit to the UK of up to six months
  • can be completed up to 3 months before travelling to the UK
  • must be completed a minimum of 48 hours before departure to the UK.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • Your passport
  • Travel details for your journey to the UK
