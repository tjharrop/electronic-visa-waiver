Electronic Visa Waiver

If you have a passport from Qatar, Kuwait, the United Arab Emirates or Oman you can travel to the UK using an electronic visa waiver.

An electronic visa waiver:

  • can be used for a single visit to the UK of up to 6 months
  • can be used for tourism, business or study purposes
  • is free
  • can be completed online

You must complete the online form at least 48 hours before you travel to the UK. You can complete it 3 months before you travel.

If you are travelling as a family or group, you need to fill in a separate form for each person.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • your passport and those of any family members travelling with you
  • travel details
  • details of where you will be staying in the UK
