Electronic visa waiver

You don’t need a visa for a visit to the UK of up to 6 months if you hold a passport of one of the following countries:

  • Kuwait
  • Oman
  • Qatar
  • United Arab Emirates

If you hold one of these passports, you can complete a simple online form to get permission to visit the UK. This is a free service known as an electronic visa waiver (EVW).

You can use an EVW to visit the UK once, for up to 6 months. If you want to make multiple trips to the UK, you must complete an EVW for each visit.

You can use an EVW to visit for:

  • tourism
  • a business trip
  • study

You cannot use an EVW to:

  • work
  • get married or enter into a civil partnership in the UK

You must upload a scan of your passport in the online form. You don’t have to provide biometrics or visit a visa application centre.

To complete the form, you will need:

  • your current passport
  • an address in the UK where you will be staying
  • details of your journey to and from the UK (including departure and arrival dates and times)

You must complete the online form at least 48 hours before your trip to the UK.

If you are travelling in a group, each group member must get an EVW for the trip.
