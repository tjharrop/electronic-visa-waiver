Upload a copy of your passport information page

To make your journey to the UK easier we ask you to provide us with a copy of your passport information page.

This is the page with your photo, name, date of birth and other information.

The image needs to clearly show all the information on the page.

You can use your smartphone to take a photo of your passport information page.

You can also use an image from your computer, or a picture scanner.

When you upload your passport we will:

  • ✔ use it to check the information you have provided matches the information on your passport
  • ✔ contact you if the information is incorrect

 

✗ If you do not provide a copy of your passport information page, we can not check your information is correct.
