Upload an image of your passport picture page

To make your journey to the UK easier we ask you to provide us with a copy of your passport picture page. This is the page with your photo, name, date of birth and other information.

If you have a photo, or a scan of your passport picture page, you can upload it here.

The benefits of uploading your passport are:

  • • the information you provide can be verified and any mistakes can be fixed.
  • • you will receive e-mail confirmation with an EVW number
I don't have an image of my passport

If you do not have an image of your passport picture page you can still use this service.

It is important that you enter the information requested exactly as written on your passport.

If you do not, you may not be allowed to board the flight to the UK.
