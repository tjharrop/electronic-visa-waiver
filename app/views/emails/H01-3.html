Electronic Visa Waiver application received

Dear A Name,

We have received your Electronic Visa Waiver (EVW) application.

Your application reference is XXXXXXXXXX.

This is not your EVW number.

What happens next?

We will now check your details before we issue your EVW.

Everything must be exactly the same as what is on your passport. Otherwise you will get an email asking for the correct information.​

When will you get your EVW?

You will receive your EVW within 24 hours after making the application.

You will get an email once your details are confirmed. It will have a link to download your EVW.

Changing your details

Email evw-details-change@ukvi.gov.uk if you need to change your EVW details​, eg if your UK arrival date has changed.

Any changes must be made at least 48 hours before you depart for the UK.
